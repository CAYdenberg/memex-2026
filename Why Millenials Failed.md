
Because we never tried.

We were never raised to bring down the system.

We were raised to recognize that the system is ill, but to think that we could win within it anyway.

Even as teenagers, with all that energy of youth, we wanted that game and that CD more than we wanted to understand the message of rebellion in the lyrics, we wanted to be the hero in the game more than we wanted apply the heroism of the game to our own shitty world.

We failed. We are the adults in the room, we made the internet and the culture that runs on top of it, and instead of using that democratized publishing platform to argue for reason or fairness, we demanded:

"Here we are now, entertain us."

We never really wanted information to be free (as in *libre*) we just didn't want to pay for it. We never wanted science, engineering or math to understand the cosmos or to create something of value, we just wanted good pay and job security. And we didn't even get that, because it was always a con.
