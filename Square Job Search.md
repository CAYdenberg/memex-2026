## Standard cover letter

To whom it may concern,

I am a full-stack developer who has been building the web for eleven years. I have worked for graphics design agencies, for product companies, and as a freelancer, where I was often the sole developer and responsible for everything from design to database. I have worked with a wide variety of frontend and backend frameworks, understand the problems they solve, and have seen their strengths and weaknesses at scale.

I also have extensive familiarity with academic workplaces. I have a graduate degree, and spent a large amount of my life around universities from 1999 until 2014 (this includes four years at SFU). I've experienced the pressures that academic workers are under first-hand, and am uniquely positioned to bring a user-centric approach to building an in-house application.

Most importantly, I have a long history of productive relationships with designers, product managers, and users. When I was freelancing, I was successful because I worked constantly with people from a variety of technical backgrounds to understand their pain points. This has given me a keen sense of UX design and an understanding of how to put the user first.

Yours,
Casey Ydenberg
